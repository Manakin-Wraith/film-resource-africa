01 — Distribution

Everbright Brings Open-Air Cinema to Dar es Salaam

A producer/distributor based in Tanzania is launching an Open Air Screening initiative this June/July — backed by funding received at EFM – Berlinale. They’re sourcing feature films (60–90 min) in comedy, romance, and light-hearted genres. Any language except Swahili. Universal audiences.

“I’m looking for films suitable for universal audiences — stories with moral themes that work under the stars.”

— Everbright, Dar es Salaam

03 — Distribution Tech

Watchroom Launches ‘Social Cinema’ for African Creators

Watchroom has built a distribution platform that transforms passive streaming into a global “Opening Night” experience. 10,000+ viewers watch in perfect sync with real-time interaction. They’re selecting 3 independent production partners for their Q2 2026 pilot.
