1. Capture local search traffic for film opportunities in each African country
2. Establish Film Resource Africa as the definitive resource for African filmmaking
3. Drive targeted traffic from filmmakers seeking country-specific opportunities
4. Improve overall domain authority through comprehensive content coverage

## Phase 2: Page Structure & Template

### Core Page Template
Each location page should follow this structure:

```markdown
# Film Opportunities in [Country Name]

## Overview
- Brief introduction to the country's film industry
- Key statistics (number of annual productions, major studios, notable filmmakers)
- Cultural context and storytelling traditions

## Current Opportunities ([Month Year])
### 🎬 Film Festivals
- List of upcoming festivals with deadlines
- Submission requirements and fees
- Past winners/notable selections

### 💰 Funding & Grants
- Government funding programs
- Private sector grants
- International development funds
- Co-production opportunities

### 🎓 Training & Labs
- Screenwriting workshops
- Directing fellowships
- Technical training programs
- Mentorship opportunities

### 🏢 Industry Resources
- Film commissions
- Production companies
- Equipment rental houses
- Post-production facilities

### 🤝 Networking Events
- Film markets
- Pitch forums
- Industry conferences
- Meetups and workshops

## Success Stories
- Case studies of successful filmmakers from the country
- Award-winning films and their journey
- Breakout talents to watch

## Practical Information
- Visa requirements for international filmmakers
- Local filming permits and regulations
- Tax incentives and rebates
- Safety considerations

## Upcoming Deadlines
- Next 3 months of critical deadlines
- Priority applications (highlighted)
- Rolling opportunities

## Subscribe for Updates
- Country-specific newsletter signup
- Deadline alert system
- Local opportunity notifications
```

## Phase 3: Content Development Workflow

### Research Phase (Per Country)
1. **Database Query:** Extract all opportunities tagged with the country
2. **Government Sources:** Research national film boards and ministries
3. **Industry Reports:** Analyze local film industry reports
4. **Success Stories:** Interview local filmmakers (3-5 per country)
5. **Regulatory Research:** Compile filming permits and tax incentives

### Content Creation
1. **Writer Assignment:** Assign to writer familiar with the region
2. **First Draft:** Complete template with researched data
3. **Fact Check:** Verify all opportunity details and deadlines
4. **Local Review:** Send to local filmmaker for feedback
5. **SEO Optimization:** Add target keywords and meta tags
6. **Publish & Index:** Publish and submit to search engines

### Maintenance Schedule
- **Weekly:** Update opportunity status (open/closed)
- **Monthly:** Add new opportunities and remove expired ones
- **Quarterly:** Refresh success stories and industry updates
- **Annually:** Comprehensive review and rewrite

## Phase 4: Technical Implementation

### URL Structure
```
/film-opportunities/[country-slug]
/film-opportunities/nigeria
/film-opportunities/south-africa
/film-opportunities/kenya
```

### SEO Elements
- **Title Tag:** `Film Opportunities in [Country] | Film Resource Africa`
- **Meta Description:** `Comprehensive guide to film festivals, grants, labs, and industry resources in [Country]. Updated [Month Year].`
- **H1:** `Film Opportunities in [Country]`
- **Schema Markup:** 
  - `LocalBusiness` for country page
  - `FAQPage` for common questions
  - `Event` for upcoming deadlines
  - `BreadcrumbList` for navigation

### Internal Linking Strategy

2. **From Directory:** Country filters and tags
3. **Between Countries:** "Neighboring countries" section
4. **From Blog:** Country-specific case studies
5. **From Newsletter:** Country spotlight features
